01
Identify Scalable Sectors & Partners

We begin with rigorous market analysis — identifying sectors with the largest financing gaps, strongest social return potential, and most credible delivery partners. We prioritise models proven at small scale and ready for structured capital to accelerate growth.

02
Design Capital Structures

We design bespoke capital structures — layering concessional first-loss capital, mezzanine tranches, and senior debt to optimise risk-adjusted returns for each investor class. Every structure is built with capital efficiency and replicability in mind.

03
Align Stakeholders

Blended finance transactions require precise alignment across DFIs, philanthropies, commercial investors, and deployment partners. We manage this complexity — negotiating terms, resolving conflicts, and building the trust infrastructure that makes transactions close.

04–05
Execute Deployment & Enable Scale

We oversee capital deployment through active transaction management, monitor outcomes against impact frameworks, and document learnings openly. Over time, we convert successful transactions into market templates — turning one-off deals into industry infrastructure.

Illustrative Structures

How We Structure Transactions

Representative transaction archetypes across our focus sectors — illustrating our structuring approach and deployment methodology.

Financial Inclusion · NBFC
Blended NBFC Lending Facility
Illustrative Structure
The Problem
Commercial lenders unwilling to provide growth capital to mid-tier NBFCs serving rural borrowers — perceived credit risk too high.
Structure Designed
First-loss tranche (20%) from foundation capital de-risks senior debt (80%) from commercial bank. Guarantee mechanism absorbs initial losses.
Capital Opportunity
₹50–200Cr facility unlocking 4–5x leverage on catalytic capital deployed.
Impact Potential
100,000+ new borrowers; 60% women; avg. ticket ₹25,000–50,000.
Climate · Clean Energy
Climate Financing Platform
Illustrative Structure
The Problem
Last-mile clean energy distribution lacks working capital. Commercial lenders see asset-light models as unbankable without credit enhancement.
Structure Designed
Blended debt facility with DFI anchor, concessional subordinated tranche, and commercial senior debt. Aggregation across 5+ distributors.
Capital Opportunity
₹100–500Cr platform with 3x leverage on concessional capital.
Impact Potential
500,000 rural households; 2M+ tonnes CO₂ avoided; 50,000+ livelihoods supported.
MSME · Credit Access
MSME Supply Chain Credit
Illustrative Structure
The Problem
Formal credit inaccessible to micro and small manufacturers — no credit history, informal revenue, limited collateral.
Structure Designed
Supply chain finance model co-originated with anchor corporate. Receivables-backed lending de-risks exposure. Fintech platform enables scalable underwriting.
Capital Opportunity
₹200Cr+ revolving facility with low NPL risk and strong recycling velocity.
Impact Potential
10,000+ MSMEs; 30% women-led; avg. business growth of 40% post credit access.
